Bake up a batch of these savoury muffins, starring nutty, subtly sweet celeriac, creamy walnuts and a zesty soft cheese frosting, for a delicious afternoon treat. Follow this easy celeriac recipe for an exciting new way to cook with this gorgeous seasonal root veg.
